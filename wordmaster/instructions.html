Wordmaster Instructions

  • You have a 6 x 5 matrix grid, with six different words in total, and all with five letters.
  • The last keyboard entry '⌦' on the Virtual Keyboard works as the enter key on your mobile devices.
  • If you guess an incorrect word and at the end, the guess will overwrite the last game grid space.
  • The backspace key '⌫ ' is the backspace and will keep backing up providing you have letters in the grid box spaces.
  • The keyboard icon is your Virtual Keyboard.
  • When you win you will see a confetti screen with an okay button.
  • Then you should see a 'Play Again' button, press that to start again.
  • If you run out of guesses after six attempts, you will see a game lose screen, click okay, and the correct word will display.
  • The new word resets itself daily after 24 hours.
  • Then, you will see water balloons being thrown at you, after the timeout animation, you should see a play again button.
Enjoy
